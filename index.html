Interested in using data to make a mark in the environmental conservation industry

As a graduate of the University of California, Santa Barbara with a Bachelor of Arts in Environmental Studies, I was passionate about courses such as: Environmental law, and Coastal Resources and Management. These courses sparked an interest in how we use science and law to do our part in mitigating the effects of climate change on the planet. I wish to build upon this knowledge and develop a career that uses data science, environmental science, conservation restoration, and sustainability to manage the effects of climate change.
