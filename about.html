When I'm not working, I'm doing the following things:

Traveling

Vietnam 2019: I toured north and south Vietnam with my family. I experienced Vietnam's cold, foggy mountain climate as well as the hot, sweltering rice paddies.

Australia 2019: I studied abroad in Australia for 5 months and was based out of Melbourne. On my spare time, I traveled to Sydney, camped in a van through Tasmania, and snorkeled the Great Barrier Reef with my friends. Then I solo traveled to the Whitsundays, visiting its white, sandy beaches.

Washington 2021: My sister, cousin, and I ventured to Washington to experience the city (and especially coffee) of Seattle. We had a blast eating oysters and hiking through Mount Rainier.

Australia 2022: I returned to Australia in 2022 for a big solo trip. I planned to finish the rest of the continent that I hadn't seen the first time and found myself constantly on the move. I started in Uluru, where I walked the red country and gazed at the stars. I flew over to Tasmania and sailed around the Tasman Peninsula and witness Australia's tallest sea cliffs. I then flew to Sydney where I met up with a friend before flying over to a small beach town on the other side of the country. Its name was Broome and I rode camels on the beach at sunset and saw vibrant, red sand beaches. I took a break from the country and flew over to Adelaide for some wine tasting and then hopped over to Queensland and visited Noosa.

New Zealand 2024: My next travel destination will be New Zealand! I hope to visit end of 2024 but we will see what life throws at me.

Plant Care

The pandemic was when I entered the plant community. I started off with a small desk plant and quickly got so into it that I ended up with 35 plants in the following 3 years. I dabbled into aroids, hoyas, cacti, and even carnivorous plants. I swear, the air in my bedroom never smelled fresher.
